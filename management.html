Management

Dr. VeeRoo

CEO - Kritical Health

Dr. Veeroo has had 15 years of diverse experience in the healthcare sector. He founded Kritical Health in 2016.

In 2004, Dr. Veeroo co-founded Vaatsalya Healthcare Solutions Pvt. Ltd. (www.vaatsalya.com) with the aim of promoting affordable & high quality healthcare in rural and semi urban India. In his capacity as the co-founder, Veeroo was involved in establishing and expanding the Vaatsalya network to eighteen hospitals in Tier II & Tier III towns. He was involved in putting together a team that made Vaatsalya's vision possible. By far, Vaatsalya is the only successful model in this space in India.

Dr. Veeroo is also the Founding Curator of Global Shapers Hubli Hub, which is an initiative of the World Economic Forum.

He has a medical degree from Karnataka Medical College, Hubli and MHA (Masters in Hospital Administration) from HIHM (Hinduja Institute of Healthcare Management), Hyderabad.
